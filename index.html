Education

Jan Mongwe's educational journey reflects a path marked by determination, adaptability, and a quest for fulfillment. His academic voyage began at Mantsha Primary School, where he laid the foundation for his future pursuits.

Transitioning to Kgola ka Leleme High School, Jan's commitment to excellence was evident as he graduated at the top of his class during matriculation.

Following a gap year, Jan entered the workforce, gaining valuable experience in the warehouse industry. Recognizing the importance of further education, he enrolled in Financial Management at Tshwane North College.

A year later, driven by a desire for broader horizons, Jan made the decision to shift gears and registered for a BCom Accounting program with Richfield from 2017 to 2019.

Undeterred by challenges in securing employment within the accounting field, Jan embarked on a new academic venture. In 2021, he registered for a BSc in Information Technology, showcasing his adaptability and eagerness to explore evolving career paths.

Having successfully completed his studies, Jan has transitioned into the realm of technology as a full-stack software developer.

While navigating the competitive job market, Jan is actively working on building an impressive portfolio to enhance his employment prospects. His dedication to continuous learning and resilience in the face of career shifts underscore his commitment to personal and professional growth as he forges ahead in the dynamic field of IT.
